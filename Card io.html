Blockchain Fundamental

Blockchain is a distributed database or ledger shared among a computer network's nodes. It's best known for its role in cryptocurrency systems, but its applications extend far beyond that.

  • Consensus Algorithms
  • Smart Contracts
  • Decentralized Applications (DApps)

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the branch of computer science focused on creating intelligent agents, systems that can reason, learn, and act autonomously. It encompasses a wide range of techniques and applications, from simple algorithms to complex neural networks.

  • Machine Learning
  • Deep learning
  • Natural language processing

AR/VR Development

Augmented Reality (AR) overlays digital information onto the real world, enhancing the user's perception of reality. It can be accessed through smartphones, tablets, or dedicated AR headsets. Virtual Reality (VR) immerses the user in a completely computer-generated environment, blocking out the physical world. It requires a VR headset to create a fully immersive experience.
  • AR/VR
  • Hardware
  • SDK
